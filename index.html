Rock The Vote App

The Rock the Vote App is a full stack application that incorporates user authentication and allows users to post about issues that are important to them, then like and comment on other users' posts.

NBA Playoffs App

A responsive React.js App that pulls live data from an API to display NBA scores, stats, and standings.

Axe Waiver App

Full Stack App I made for my friend who owns an Axe Throwing company. The app allows users to read and sign the liability waiver, made with React, Express, & MongoDB Atlas.

Beautifully Ugly App
